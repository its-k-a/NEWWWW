One Piece is an anime that is over 1100 episodes long. It includes an adventure between the main character luffy and adventurers he finds along the way that joins his "crew". Luffy and his crew adventures all over the sea getting into variety of battles and meeting new companions to achieve their end goal which is to find the lost one piece which no one knows what it truly is but it known to be something that gives ultimate power.


One Piece Main Characters

  • Luffy-gum gum fruit user
  • Zoro-three sword style
  • Nami-navigator
  • Usopp-sniper with slingshot
  • Sanji-black leg style
  • Chopper-doctor
  • Robin-flower flower fruit user
  • Franky-invents and repairs
  • Brook-yomi yomi fruit user
  • Jimbei-strategist

    • These characters are the main character Luffy and his crew that he finds and adventures with throughout the show.

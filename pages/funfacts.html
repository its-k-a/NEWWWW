One Piece fruits:

  1. Gomu Gomu no Mi : gum fruit
  2. Neko Neko no Mi : cat fruit
  3. Gura Gura no Mi : tremor fruit
  4. Yami Yami no Mi : dark fruit
  5. Mera Mera no Mi : flame fruit
  6. Ope Ope no Mi : control fruit
  7. Goro Goro no Mi : lightning fruit
  8. Hito Hito no Mi : human fruit
  9. Bara Bara no Mi : chop fruit
  10. Tori Tori no Mi : bird fruit
  11. Uo Uo no Mi : fish fruit used by kaido making him a dragon
  12. Magu Magu no Mi : magma fruit
  13. Mero Mero no Mi : love fruit
  14. Horo Horo no Mi : hollow fruit
  15. Nikyu Nikyu no Mi : paw fruit
  16. Doku Doku no Mi: venom fruit
  17. Suna Suna no Mi : sand fruit
  18. Hie Hie no Mi : ice fruit
  19. Pika Pika no Mi : light fruit
  20. Bari Bari no Mi : barrier fruit

Although there are increasingly more fruits than these with many unique abilities; these are the main and most important fruits used within One Piece.
